Cloudiness




There is very weak correlation observed between Cloudiness and Latitude.

Linear equation for
Northern Hemisphere: Y = 0.09X + 44.72, R-square 2.6%
Southern Hemisphere: Y = 0.03X + 47.51, R-square 0.01%
(Y =Cloudiness, X=Latitude)

As the graph above indicates, there is very little observable correlation between the latitude and Cloudiness in the atmosphere at the date of weather data (as at the June 18 2020)..
